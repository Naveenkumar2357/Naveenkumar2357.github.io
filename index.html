About the Club

The Heavy-Ion Collision Phenomenology Journal Club meets weekly to discuss relevant topics in heavy-ion collision (HIC) physics, with an emphasis on recent developments and new advances in the field.

Each week, a member presents a paper or theme, followed by a Q&A and a discussion session.

What to Expect

  • Weekly presentations on recent HIC phenomenology papers
  • Focused discussion and follow-up questions
  • Open environment for learning and collaboration
